Program Overview

Program Overview

Welcome to your first lesson of the Pharmacy Technician program! You probably have a lot of questions. How did the world of pharmacy start? What exactly is a pharmacy technician? What does a pharmacy technician do? You'll find the answers to these questions in this lesson.

But first, let's go over what you'll learn in the program as a whole. After successfully completing this program, you'll be ready to take the Certified Pharmacy Technician certification exam and begin your career as a CPhT (Certified Pharmacy Technician).

Here's an overview of the topics you'll be studying in this program:

  • Pharmacy laws and ethics
  • Pharmaceutical calculations
  • Prescriptions, labels, and errors
  • Pharmacology - basics and specific populations
  • Pharmacology - neurological body systems
  • Pharmacology - muscles, heart, and lungs body systems
  • Pharmacology - multi-body systems
  • Communications and billing

You'll also have two hands-on lab components in this program: one for retail practice, and one for hospital practice.

All right, ready to get started? On the next page we'll begin by looking at the learning objectives for our first lesson.
